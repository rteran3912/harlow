## Medication Alerts & Interactions

### TIMING & FOOD RULES
- **Metronidazole** — Give WITH a small amount of food (reduces GI upset)
- **Metoclopramide** — Give 15-30 min BEFORE meals on empty stomach (promotes motility before food arrives)
- **Cerenia (Maropitant)** — Give with a small amount of food if used (empty stomach can cause vomiting)
- **Ondansetron** — Can be given with or without food
- **Proviable probiotic** — Give with food. Separate from Metronidazole by at least 1-2 HOURS (antibiotic kills probiotic organisms)

### DO NOT COMBINE
- **Metoclopramide + Ondansetron** — Do NOT give at the same time. Both have serotonin (5-HT3) activity. If nauseous, pick ONE — don't stack both.
- **All 3 anti-emetics at once** — Do not give Metoclopramide + Ondansetron + Cerenia together. Use Cerenia OR Ondansetron as rescue on top of scheduled Metoclopramide, not all three.

### STEROID LOAD — DISCUSS WITH VET
- Harlow is receiving THREE corticosteroids: systemic Dex SP injection + ophthalmic Neopolydex (contains dexamethasone) + ophthalmic Difluprednate (potent fluorinated steroid). Ophthalmic steroids ARE systemically absorbed in dogs. Difluprednate can cause HPA axis suppression even from eye drops alone. Confirm with vet that they are aware of all three steroid sources.

### NSAID + STEROID CONCERN (Eyes)
- Bromfenac (ophthalmic NSAID) with ophthalmic steroids (Neopolydex, Difluprednate) increases risk of corneal healing problems. Not contraindicated but requires monitoring.

### EYE DROP SPACING
- Wait minimum **5 minutes between each different drop** in the same eye
- Suggested order: Dorzolamide first (glaucoma med, best absorption first) → Bromfenac → Neopolydex → Difluprednate (steroids last, thicker formulations)

### STORAGE
- **Dorzolamide** — Room temp, do NOT refrigerate. Protect from light. Discard 42 days after opening.
- **Difluprednate** — Room temp, do NOT freeze. Keep in carton (light-sensitive).
- **Cerenia blister pack** — Keep sealed until use (moisture/light-sensitive).
- **B-12 injection** — Protect from light.
